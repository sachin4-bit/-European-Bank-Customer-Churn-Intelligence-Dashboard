📊 Key Insights

✅ Overall churn rate: 18.8%
✅ Germany showed the highest churn risk at 27.1%
✅ Female customers had higher churn than male customers
✅ Customers with 3+ products showed critical churn risk
✅ Inactive members churned significantly more than active members
